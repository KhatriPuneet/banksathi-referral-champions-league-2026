Frequently Asked
What is the Referral Champions League?
A 45 Day referral contest by BankSathi where you get chance to win exiting prizes + earn 10% commission on every referral's earnings.
Is the 10% commission on top of my regular payout?
Yes — the 10% referral commission is an extra bonus on top of your regular lead payouts. It doesn't replace anything.
When is a referral considered valid?
A referral is valid when the referred partner earns a minimum of ₹500 through BankSathi. Until then, it stays in "Pending or In-progress" status.
How are leaderboard rank calculated?
Rank is calculated per valid referral. The exact breakdown depends on the referral's earnings. Check the Contest Rules for the full breakdown.
How will I receive my Prize?
Once you are eligble for the prize. You will receive a confirmation call/whstapp/email notification from BankSathi team.
